Stock market

The game is based on a stock market simulation where stock prices are tied to companies valuation.

Trading on the MarMix stock market

The stock market is fully configurable. Based on the game level, dividends payment, transaction costs (fixed and variable), opening auctions and interest payments can be enabled to tailor the game to each curriculum's needs.

Each market comes with a liquidity trader that simulates the invisible part of the market, bringing liquidity where needed. Liquidity traders are able to bring liquidities on the market without influencing the stocks' price.
